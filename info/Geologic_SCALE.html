Gelogical Map

Globally consistent 1:5,000,000-scale geologic map of the moon, seamlessly derived from six digitally renovated geologic maps. This type of information supports scientific research, and future geologic mapping endeavors at various scales and serves as an educational tool for those interested in lunar geology. The map includes unit contacts, geologic unit polygons, linear features, and nomenclature annotation, overlaid on shaded-relief products derived from SELENE Kaguya terrain camera stereo and LOLA altimetry data.
